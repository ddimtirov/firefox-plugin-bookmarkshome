The most important features in the BookmarksHome stylesheet are the colors and the font size. The #xxxxxx values (e.g. #ffffff in the stylesheet below) are values for colors. The best way to find the colors you like is to use some kind of color chooser. You can change the font size by changing the em values (e.g. from .8em to .7em or 1.2em) or px values of the font(-size) property. If you want to know more you can read this stylesheet tutorial.

To use one of the stylesheets on this page, just copy it from the textarea, paste it into the empty styletab of your options window, and click on apply.

Don't let yourself be discouraged from sending in your stylesheet if you don´t know how to make one as advanced as those below; finding good colors is an art in itself, so someone else´s stylesheet with only the colors changed is welcome too!
