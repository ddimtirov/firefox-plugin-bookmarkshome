Version 1.1.2
  • The folder pane has twice the height of 1.1.1's pane.
  • Selected folders can either be included or excluded.
  • Option to select subfolders for in- or exclusion. Does not apply to future subfolders. Let me know if you'd like the option to have newly created subfolders (automatically) in/excluded
  • If you want to keep your excluded folders preference (of version 1.1 or 1.1.1), you have to do the following after the first display of version 1.1.2's page:
    1. Type about:config in the location bar.
    2. Select bookmarkshome.excludedFolders, right click, and choose Copy Value.
    3. Select bookmarkshome.inOrExcludedFolders, right click, and choose Modify.
    4. Right click in the appearing popup's text field, choose Paste, and click OK.
    5. To clean up, you can right click on bookmarkshome.excludedFolders and choose Reset.
  • If you want to remove the BMHome button from your personal toolbar, you can do step 4 ([..] overlayinfo folder) of Uninstallation. This counts for Firefox too! I'll think of something better for the next version.
